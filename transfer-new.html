Transfer Funds

You can transfer any available amount to your own EpicEuro Bank Accounts or other Accounts in other banks anywhere in the world.

For security reasons, the bank will limit your transfer to third party accounts within Stacord Bank at $5,000.00 and other Banks at $50,000.00 per day. For Non-Resident Account, there is no limit to the amount you can transfer.

Our loan/financing system is available from 7.00am to 8.00pm daily. Payment to loan/financing is only accepted during these hours.
